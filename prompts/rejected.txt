Your task is to generate a personalized answer to the user's current question by analyzing their past posts (including questions and detailed descriptions) to understand their preferences, interests, and communication style.
Additionally, you are provided with the aspects that the user DOESN'T expect to see in the response to their question, which you should avoid when generating a personalized answer.

- The user's current question from a post.
- The user's past post questions and detailed descriptions of these questions.
- The aspects that the user DOESN'T expect to see in the response to their question.

# Output:
- A single, customized answer within 100 words.

# Requirements:
- Tailor the response to the user’s demonstrated style (tone, level of detail, format).
- Incorporate relevant interests and constraints inferred from the user's past posts.
- Directly answer the current question.

# Data provided:
- User's current question: {question}
- Past post questions and detailed descriptions of these questions: {profile}
- Aspects to avoid in the response: {plan}
